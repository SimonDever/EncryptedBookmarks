Create your encryption password

Encrypted Bookmarks will generate a public and private key to, respectively, encrypt and decrypt your bookmarks. This password will encrypt your private key.
Please note:

  • Recovery is impossible if you forget this password.
  • This password cannot be changed.
  • Make this password long to protect yourself against brute-force attacks.
